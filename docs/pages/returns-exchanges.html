Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn’t right, we’re here to help with returns or exchanges.

Window: requests must be initiated within 30 days of delivery for items purchased on purvanti.com.

Condition: items should be unopened and in original packaging unless reporting a defect or shipping damage.

Start a request: email hello@purvanti.com with your order number, the item you wish to return or exchange, and the reason for the request.

Shipping: return instructions and any applicable labels will be provided after approval. Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Exchanges: when available, we’ll ship the replacement once the return is in transit. If the item is out of stock, we’ll offer a refund or store credit.

Refund timing: refunds are processed to the original payment method; banks and payment partners may take additional time to post funds.

For support at any step, reach us at hello@purvanti.com or 1 (720) 864 0086.
