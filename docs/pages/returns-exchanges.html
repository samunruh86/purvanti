Returns & Exchanges

At Purvanti, we want every order to meet your expectations. If something isn’t right, we’re here to help make the process clear, fair, and straightforward.

Return Eligibility

Return or exchange requests must be initiated within 30 days of delivery for purchases made directly through purvanti.com.

  • Items must be unopened and in their original packaging
  • Products showing signs of use are not eligible for return
  • Defective or damaged items may qualify for an exception

How to Start a Return or Exchange

To request a return or exchange, please email hello@purvanti.com with the following information:

  • Your order number
  • The item(s) you wish to return or exchange
  • A brief explanation of the reason for your request

Our support team will review your request and provide next steps if approved.

Return Shipping

Once approved, return instructions and any applicable shipping details will be provided. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error or damage on our part.

Exchanges

If you’re requesting an exchange and the replacement item is available, we’ll ship it once the returned item is in transit. If the replacement is unavailable, we’ll offer a refund or store credit based on your preference.

Refunds

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method after the returned item is received and inspected. Processing times vary by bank or payment provider and may take several business days to appear on your statement.
