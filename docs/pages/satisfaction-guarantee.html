Satisfaction Guarantee

Your experience matters to us. If you are not satisfied with a Purvanti product, we’ll work with you to make it right.

Eligibility: applies to purchases made directly from purvanti.com within 30 days of delivery. Products must be in their original packaging unless there was a defect or shipping damage.

How to start: contact our support team at hello@purvanti.com with your order number, product details, and a brief description of the issue.

Resolution options: replacement, store credit, or refund depending on the situation. We may request photos for quality review and carrier claims.

Exclusions: final-sale items and bulk/wholesale orders may not qualify. Misuse, improper storage, or damage after delivery is not covered.

Processing: approved refunds are issued to the original payment method; processing times vary by bank or payment provider.

If you have questions about our guarantee, reach out and we’ll respond quickly.
