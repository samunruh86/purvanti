Satisfaction Guarantee

Your experience matters. If something about your order isn’t right, our team will work with you to resolve it quickly and fairly.

Coverage

Our Satisfaction Guarantee applies to purchases made directly through purvanti.com and initiated within 30 days of delivery.

Eligibility

  • Items should be in original packaging when possible
  • Products that arrive damaged or defective may qualify for an exception
  • We may request photos to support quality review or carrier claims

How to Start a Request

Email info@purvanti.com and include:

  • Your order number
  • The product name(s)
  • A brief description of the issue

Our support team will reply with next steps and resolution options.

Resolution Options

Depending on the situation, we may offer:

  • A replacement (when available)
  • Store credit
  • A refund to the original payment method

Exclusions

This guarantee does not cover issues resulting from misuse, improper storage, or damage that occurs after delivery. Final-sale items may be ineligible where noted at purchase.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times vary by bank or payment provider and may take several business days to appear on your statement.
